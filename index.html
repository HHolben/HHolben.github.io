About Me

Hello World! Welcome to my website. My name is Henry Holben, I like to make things and write stuff. I am currently pursing an electrical engineering degree with a multimedia journalism minor at Milligan College University.


I was born and raised in Johnson City, Tennessee, baptized at First Christian Church in Johnson City, and instilled with common sense at Providence Academy (they gave it their best shot). My life goals include designing a board game and participating in a Welsh bog-snorkeling contest. I write science fiction; I occasionally play a cello, and I really, really like airplanes.
