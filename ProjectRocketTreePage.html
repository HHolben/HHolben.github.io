Rocket Tree

My family has a New Years eve tradition that I look forward to every year. We take out our used Christmas tree, stand it up on a wood stand or on our burn pile, load it up with fireworks, and "blow it up" at midnight. Usually we have family and friends over. My parents and their friends started the tradition before I was born and over the years the idea of "launching a tree" instead of blowing up the tree kept coming up.

So we had to try it.
